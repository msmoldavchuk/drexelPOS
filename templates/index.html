What is POS?

"Welcome to the world of efficient and organized studying! We are excited to introduce you to our new Plan of Study Generator web page, designed to help you take control of your studies and achieve academic success. With our innovative tool, you can easily create a personalized study plan that fits your schedule, and balancing your coursework. Whether you're a CCI student or someone looking to improve their study habits, our POS is the solution you've been searching for. So why wait? Start taking the first step towards better grades and a brighter future, today!"

How does POS work?

This generator will use data parsed from Drexel's course catalog and term master schedule. Then an algorithm will rank courses on importance and fill up an individual's quarters to create a plan of study.

What can we do?

Features of this product are that a user can input their co-op cycle, APs, planned graduation year and minors they are interested in. This will be shown on a website and it will include the plan of study and an individual's degree requirements. In addition, within the plan of study certain courses will be highlighted to emphasize that they are the most important to take.

Why, us?

It will provide an interactive and user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to modify their plan and adapt it to their changing needs. So users can stay on top of their studies and avoid falling behind

Academic Planning

Transfer in Courses

In certain situations, students can take courses outside of Drexel and have them transfer back to Drexel.

Policies for transfer of undergraduate credit:

+ Transfer credit will be given for any pre-approved course for which you received a grade of "C" or better.
+ A maxium of 90 transfer credits earned at an outside institution can count towards CCI degree.
