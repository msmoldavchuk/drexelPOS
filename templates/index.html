How does POS work?

This generator will use data parsed from Drexel's course catalog and term master schedule. Then an algorithm will rank courses on importance and fill up an individual's quarters to create a plan of study.

What can we do?

Features of this product are that a user can input their co-op cycle, APs, planned graduation year and minors they are interested in. This will be shown on a website and it will include the plan of study and an individual's degree requirements. In addition, within the plan of study certain courses will be highlighted to emphasize that they are the most important to take.

Why, us?

It will provide an interactive and user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to modify their plan and adapt it to their changing needs. So users can stay on top of their studies and avoid falling behind
